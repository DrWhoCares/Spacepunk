\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A \mbox{\hyperlink{class_b_box}{B\+Box}} (short for bounding box) is an entity \mbox{\hyperlink{class_component}{Component}} that adds collision to an entity. A \mbox{\hyperlink{class_b_box}{B\+Box}} at the root of an entity that is named \char`\"{}physics\char`\"{} will become a driver for the entity A \mbox{\hyperlink{class_b_box}{B\+Box}} with mass 0 is static and should not be moved. A \mbox{\hyperlink{class_b_box}{B\+Box}} with negative mass becomes kinematic and moves with \char`\"{}game-\/style\char`\"{} physics, but will still stop when encountering other bboxes. A \mbox{\hyperlink{class_b_box}{B\+Box}} with positive mass becomes a rigid body and will be automatically affected by gravity.
